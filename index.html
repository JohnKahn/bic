About

The Big Idea Competition is UT Dallas' largest startup pitch competition. Undergraduate and Graduate students from all disciplines compete for $20,000 in cash and scholarship prizes and the chance to pitch in front of their peers and a panel of esteemed judges.

Prize Categories

  • First, Second, and Third Places

  • Biggest Idea / Most Innovative

  • Best Social Enterprise

  • Best Undergraduate Idea

  • Diversity

FAQ

  • Will Guy Kawasaki be doing Q/A?

    He will not be able to stay for a Q/A session.

  • Will Guy sign my book?

    Guy will not be doing book signings, but a signed book comes with the Preffered Admission ticket.

  • Can I have a picture with Guy?

    There will not be an opportunity to take a picture with Guy.

  • If student tickets are sold out, can I still come?

    If student tickets are no longer available, then you can purchase a General Admission or Preferred Seating ticket. There will also be a wait-list line at the event.

  • Do I have to pay for parking?

    Parking information is coming soon.
